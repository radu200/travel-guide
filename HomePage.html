Paris, France

Paris: it's not easy arriving alone in one of the world's most romantic cities. Everywhere you look there seem to be couples strolling, enjoying drinks, dinner or the sights. But you can have a brilliant time in Paris by yourself. It's a remarkable city - very easy to navigate on foot as well as by Metro - but full of unexpected delights which you don't have to be with anyone else to have a truly memorable time

Paris is a beautiful city. Making the most of the scenery, I never catch the metro to get to and from places that I want to visit. The most beautiful and scenic way to one side of Paris to the other, is a walk along the River Seine. This guide is my way of expressing my absolute, unconditional love for Paris. There is always something new and exciting to see here, whether you have been here once or several times. It's a comfortable place, where the people are friendly and the streets are safe for nighttime walks to the Eiffel Tower and a late night cafe visit.
